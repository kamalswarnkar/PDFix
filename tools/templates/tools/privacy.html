Privacy Policy

PDFix provides free online tools for working with PDF files. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

File Processing

Files uploaded to PDFix are processed automatically on the server in order to perform the requested tool operation such as merging, splitting, or converting.

File Deletion

Uploaded files and generated output files are automatically deleted from our server within 30 minutes of being created. Files are removed on the next request after they exceed this window — no files are retained beyond 30 minutes.

No Permanent Storage

PDFix does not permanently store your documents.

Cookies

PDFix may use minimal cookies required for functionality such as download handling.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements to the service.

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
