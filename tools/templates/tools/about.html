About PDFix

PDFix provides free and easy-to-use online tools for working with PDF documents. Our goal is to make everyday PDF tasks simple, fast, and accessible to everyone.

With PDFix you can merge, split, compress, convert, and manage your PDF files directly from your browser without installing any software.

All files uploaded to PDFix are processed automatically and deleted shortly after processing to protect your privacy.
