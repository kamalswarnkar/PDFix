Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFix free to use?

Yes. All PDFix tools are completely free to use online with no signup required.

Are my files secure?

Yes. Files uploaded to PDFix are processed automatically and deleted shortly after processing.

Do you store uploaded files?

No. Files are temporarily processed and automatically removed from the server to protect your privacy.

Do these tools work on mobile?

Yes. PDFix tools work on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.
